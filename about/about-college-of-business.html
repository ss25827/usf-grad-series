USF College of Business

The USF College of Business prepares students to contribute to and take leading positions in business and society. Our teaching, scholarship, and service link theory and practice to benefit the University and the communities it serves.

USF's College of Business does more than simply disseminate knowledge - it transforms minds while arming students with the skills and knowledge to take leadership positions in business and society. To us, the business world is our classroom. Our metropolitan location provides rich opportunities to integrate real-world experiences into classroom learning through internships and jobs, speakers, and service-learning projects.

Our Priorities are Simple:

  • Pursuing academic excellence by attracting and retaining the very best faculty
  • Ensuring student success in a progressive environment
  • Enhancing student experiences through innovative programs
  • Creating centers of excellence where students learn from faculty who practice what they teach

Information Systems and Decision Sciences

Reflecting the changing IT landscape, the needs of employers, and using insight gleaned from a panel of corporate partners, the MIS curriculum ensures graduates have both business acumen and practical information systems knowledge. MIS majors blend technical and business management skills required in today’s global marketplace.

Our faculty has strong ties to industry in the Tampa Bay area. With a group of top IT executives from the Tampa Bay area as members of the advisory board, we strive to make our curriculum current in the face of ever changing IT landscape.
