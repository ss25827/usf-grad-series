Business Intelligence Focus

What is Business Intelligence?

Business Intelligence (B.I.) is a strategic function involving Data Warehousing, Analytics, Trend Tracking, Forecasting, Competitive Intelligence, and Consumer Data Research using advanced database and statistical packages. B.I. uses technologies, processes, and applications to analyze and establish a pattern of better business decision making. Enterprises are finding ways to leverage these growing technologies to gain a competitive advantage.

This focus equips you with skills to understand business priorities and develop an integrated strategy for aligning people and business processes. Utilizing information technology to solve business challenges and opportunities enables you to champion and accomplish organizational objectives.
