Raymond James

Our corporate vision, as outlined by our CEO, is to be the premier alternative to Wall Street. One of our strategic initiatives for supporting this vision is to invest in our people. At Raymond James, we believe in providing the greatest opportunities for both career and personal development. It all adds up to total job satisfaction, and we’re doing it through our culture of independence. Discover what it means to be part of the Raymond James family.

Microsoft

You have unique experiences, skills and passions—and we believe you can bring them all to Microsoft for a rich, rewarding career and lifestyle that will surprise you with its breadth and potential. Just imagine the excitement and satisfaction of what you can do, where you can go, and the difference you can make with the resources of Microsoft behind you.

Citigroup

Citi works tirelessly to serve individuals, communities, institutions and nations. With 200 years of experience meeting the world's toughest challenges and seizing its greatest opportunities, we strive to create the best outcomes for our clients and customers with financial solutions that are simple, creative and responsible. An institution connecting over 1,000 cities, 160 countries and millions of people, we are your global bank; we are Citi.

JPMorgan Chase

At JPMorgan Chase, we believe in the power of people and that one person can significantly influence our industry and enrich the livelihoods of the clients, shareholders, employees, and communities we so proudly serve. We also believe in "strength in numbers." That is why we embrace both individuality and teamwork — because this fusion of individual recognition and teamwork creates a corporate culture. Working in our collaborative environment, you will share and cultivate innovative ideas that will contribute to the development of sound business solutions. You will provide your unique perspective, enabling our entire organization to address complexities of the ever-changing financial and banking industries. You will also participate in numerous training programs alongside your colleagues. Together, you and your co-workers will gain the knowledge and skills needed to succeed at every stage of your career.

Tech Data

Tech Data pioneered the IT distribution industry more than 35 years ago. Today, across the world . . . around the globe . . . technology isn’t slowing down, and neither are we! Tech Data plays an indispensable role in getting technology from those who invent it to those who sell it. Through economic ups and downs, Tech Data continues to grow. Thanks to a world-class executive management team and dedicated employees, Tech Data is an established global distribution leader and a respected presence in our communities. Our success is about teamwork. It’s about partnership. It's about commitment. It's about our passion for winning. We expect nothing more and accept nothing less.

DTCC

At the Depository Trust & Clearing Corporation (DTCC), we have a persistent vision of our future in a rapidly changing, globalized marketplace. It's a vision built on the resiliency of a trusted service organization that provides certainty and continuity within today's financial system. A vision built on highly motivated, high-performing people who demonstrate industry expertise, flawless integrity, and determined talent.
