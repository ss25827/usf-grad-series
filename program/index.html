Program Details

Successful technologists in any field share a few key attributes: collection, organization, integration, analysis, and management of data and information. For the operation and management of modern enterprises, these traits are not just beneficial – they are essential. Opportunities in the business world of data management are constantly expanding. Now, more than ever, there is an increased demand for database managers to build strong foundations for the development of strategic business systems.

With decades of experience preparing graduates for careers in the technology industry, the University of South Florida has a unique perspective on the skills you are going to need to excel in the business. We have applied that knowledge in crafting the curriculum for our online Master of Science in Management Information Systems (MS/MIS) degree program.

Although the master’s degree covers a number of advanced topics – Operations and Supply Chain Process, Advanced Systems Analysis and Design, Statistical Data Mining, and more – your education will not just be traditional lectures. By interacting with international businesses and a cohort of peers from around the world in a highly interactive web-based learning environment, you will build your professional network and hone your communication skills, as you learn firsthand how the industry’s leaders operate.

Through case studies and in-depth research, you will gain a better understanding of what makes a successful business thrive. And throughout the program, industry leading-faculty will share their experiences, giving even greater insight into how they have achieved success and overcome challenges.

Near the end of the Management Information System's degree program, you will begin the capstone Project Management course using the skills and knowledge that you have obtained throughout your courses. This course details fundamental issues for managing projects and creates an understanding of competing demands in information technology environments. Depending on prior experience, students can prepare and take exams leading to Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM) and Project Management Professional (PMP) designations.

Why MIS at USF?

Online Format

Developed to help working I.T. professionals build upon existing skills and achieve career success by advancing their education, this program offers a fully online experience, giving you flexibility to continue working, avoid employment gaps, and eliminate campus visits. An online delivery format means wherever you are, the classroom follows. Free from the constraints of physical meetings, you can work around busy schedules and never miss class. This program mirrors the rigorous curriculum of the on-campus program, and our cutting edge technology enables you to pursue your degree with a cohort of peers from all over the world in a highly interactive web-based learning environment.

Certification

In addition, you will have the opportunity to receive a joint SAS/USF Certificate in Analytics and Business Intelligence. Certificates are awarded to students using SAS Enterprise Miner or an equivalent SAS analytics package in the Data Mining and Statistical Data Mining courses. Software tools will be provided free for class uses by the SAS Institute. In order to earn the certificate, students must complete the following courses with a GPA of 3.0 or higher:

  • ISM 6136 Data Mining
  • ISM 6137 Statistical Data Mining
  • ISM 6218 Advanced Database Systems
  • ISM 6208 Data Warehousing
