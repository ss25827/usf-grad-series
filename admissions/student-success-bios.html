Student Success Specialist

Lisa Orr, M.Ed.

Greetings and welcome to the University of South Florida!

I look forward to working with you as your student success specialist for the Online Master of Science in Management Information Systems program. I am here to assist you with your enrollment into this program, so please feel free to contact me at any time.

A little about my background . . . I have lived in the Tampa Bay area since coming to USF as an undergraduate student in 1983. I later earned my MA degree in Education at USF, while working as a graduate assistant, as well as obtaining a Florida state teacher’s certification.

For the past ten years, I have worked as a program coordinator with USF University College, Professional and Workforce Development. Here I gained a wealth of experience in working with professionals from all walks of life who strive to further their knowledge, skills and credentials – an ever more valuable commodity in today’s competitive environment as you well know.

In the many years I have been at USF, I have met wonderful people and learned a great deal about how the university works. At the same time I have worked very closely with the public and corporate world, maintaining a constant understanding of workforce needs and current trends across industries. I look forward to putting this knowledge to use in helping you have an experience here at USF that will be positive and life changing.

I would be happy to speak with you and answer any questions you may have at this time about the USF MIS program. You can call 813-974-5565 or 1-855-756-3681 (toll free) or email lisaorr@usf.edu .
