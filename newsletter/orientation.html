Dear Student:

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you into the Online Master of Science in Management Information Systems program at the University of South Florida (USF). I strongly encourage you to meet with me and my faculty colleagues online, Thursday, August 15th at 12:00 pm for a live orientation and welcome to the USF Bulls community.

During the orientation, you will see a course demonstration, hear from the faculty, and get a better grasp of the program sequence. You will also receive your student handbook. From the convenience of your home or office you can take advantage of the opportunity to ask questions of all faculty members present about the program or resolve any immediate concerns over the registration process.

I hope you will put your best foot forward and stay actively involved in your education process over the next two years. I encourage your inquires and look forward to meeting you online!
