Tuition Payment Information

USF does not mail out tuition bills. To find your account balance:

  1. Log on to OASIS
  2. On the Main Menu select "Student"
  3. On the Student Menu select “Tuition & Fees”
  4. On the Tuition, Fees & Payment menu select “View Account Summary and Pay My Bill”

Your current account balance is shown near the top of the page. All charges and payments are summarized by the term in which they were made. For payment options, please view the cashier’s website.

For questions or concerns about your tuition and costs, please contact your student success specialist.
