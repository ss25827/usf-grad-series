Registration

How to register for classes

This registration process applies to students interested in taking graduate series classes. This process should be used by degree-seeking, non-degree seeking in-state and out-of-state students. For questions about the graduate series registration process, please contact your student success specialist by phone: (813) 974-1011 or 1-855-756-3681; or email lisaorr@usf.edu.

To register for graduate series courses:

  1. Complete the Registration Worksheet
  2. FAX the form to 813-974-7061 or email lisaorr@usf.edu.
  3. After 3-5 business days, you may go to OASIS to verify your registration. You will not receive an e-mail confirmation.
  4. Pay for Courses in OASIS.** Click here to access OASIS and here for important payment deadlines.

Are you a non-degree seeking student at USF?

  1. Complete the USF Non-Degree Status Application
  2. Register for a course using the process outlined above.

** Please note that you may see charges exceeding the cost of your program fees. A waiver will be issued to remove the excess charges. However, you MUST pay your expected charges by the stated deadline to avoid late fees and the possibility of having your classes canceled.
